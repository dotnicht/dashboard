FAQ

What is CRS?
The CRS is a secure decentralized cryptocurrency that was developed by the Crystals team and serves as the sole payment method used within the CRYSTALS Ecosystem.

How referral program works?
Instead of rewarding you for inviting new participants in tokens, we have a much better proposition: share your referral link among friend and receive 3% of their deposits in original cryptocurrency (BTC of ETH)

What is a schedule of token sale?
Crowdsale of CRYSTASL (CRS) will be conducted in two stages:
  • Pre-sale May 29, 2018 – June 12, 2018
  • Token sale: June 19, 2018 – July 19, 2018

Do I need to pass KYC?
Yes, you will not be able to receive CRS tokens without successful KYC.

Do I need to open an Ethereum wallet to participate in pre-sale/token sale?
Yes, you need to have your Ethereum wallet in order to collect tokens. You can find an instruction here or address your questions to our friendly support.

What if I already have an Ethereum wallet and want to have CRS tokens on it?
You can use any of your ERC-20 supported wallets.

When will tokens be distributed
Tokens will be distributed after the end of the token sale. Please note: Tokens will be distributed ONLY to users who successfully passed KYC.

How CRS Token will be created?
The CRS Token is an ERC20 Token created on the Ethereum blockchain.

What payment options do you have?
BTC, ETH, Wire Transfer

What limits does token sale have?
